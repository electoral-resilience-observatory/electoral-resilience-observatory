About the Observatory

The Electoral Resilience Observatory provides structured, evidence-oriented analysis of how election management bodies and democratic institutions respond to disruption, uncertainty, and emerging risks.

What we analyze

EMB reports, parliamentary oversight reports, election observer reports, post-election reviews, legal reforms, operational disruptions, and public confidence issues.

What we extract

Threats, resilience strategies, institutional adaptations, organizational learning, lessons learned, recommendations, and policy implications.

What we produce

Executive summaries, thematic coding, comparative tables, dashboards, heat maps, scorecards, case studies, and downloadable datasets.

Analytical Framework

The Observatory organizes electoral resilience analysis around four categories of threats and institutional responses.

🌍

Environmental Threats

Public health shocks, natural hazards, postal disruption, fiscal pressure, physical security risks, and external operating conditions.

👥

Societal Threats

Turnout decline, voter confusion, polarization, trust erosion, accessibility barriers, and public confidence challenges.

💻

Technological & Informational Threats

Cyberattacks, misinformation, disinformation, AI-generated deepfakes, electoral roll data risks, and digital platform vulnerabilities.

🏛️

Institutional & Logistical Threats

Staffing, funding, procurement, late legal reform, training, ICT readiness, accommodation, logistics, and operational delivery pressures.

Methods

The Observatory uses a transparent coding framework to identify threats, responses, learning, adaptation, and recommendations in election-related reports.

01

Collect Reports

Gather EMB reports, parliamentary reviews, election observer reports, and post-election evaluations.

02

Code Evidence

Extract evidence of electoral threats, operational disruptions, organizational learning, and institutional responses.

03

Structure Findings

Organize coded findings into comparative tables, matrices, indicators, and policy-relevant summaries.

04

Visualize Resilience

Produce dashboards, heat maps, scorecards, timelines, and comparative visualizations.
